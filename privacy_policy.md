# Privacy Policy for Multi-Calendar App

**Last Updated:** May 12, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to Multi-Calendar App ("we," "our," or "us"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### No Personal Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.** Our app is designed with your privacy as a priority. Specifically:

- We do **not** require user accounts or registration
- We do **not** collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data
- We do **not** track your usage or behavior
- We do **not** use analytics or crash reporting services that collect personal data
- We do **not** use third-party advertising networks

### Local Data Storage Only

All data you create within the app is stored **locally on your device only**:

- **Reminders:** Saved using Hive database on your device
- **Age Records:** Saved using Hive database on your device  
- **Bookmarks:** Saved locally on your device
- **App Preferences:** Your language and settings choices are stored locally

**Important:** We have no access to this data. It remains solely on your device.

## How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any personal information, we don't use your data for:
- Marketing or advertising
- Analytics or research
- Sharing with third parties
- Sending notifications or emails

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your data (reminders, age records, preferences) is stored using Hive, a secure local database
- Data never leaves your device
- You have full control to delete this data at any time by:
  - Clearing app data in your device settings
  - Uninstalling the app
  - Using the "Delete" functions within the app

### No Cloud Storage
- We do not use cloud storage services
- We do not sync your data across devices
- We do not have servers that store your information

## Email Feature

The app includes an optional email feature that allows you to share age records via email:

- **When you use this feature:** The app opens your default email client
- **What is shared:** Only the specific age record data you choose to share
- **No data collection:** We do not store email addresses or email content
- **No servers involved:** Emails are sent directly from your device through your email provider

## Permissions

Our app may request the following permissions:

- **No special permissions required** - The app works fully without any sensitive permissions

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no risk of children's information being collected or shared.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party packages that operate **entirely locally** on your device:

| Package | Purpose | Data Collection |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| Hive | Local database storage | None - works offline |
| Provider | State management | None |
| calendar_picker_ghe | Date picker UI | None |
| ethiopian_datetime | Ethiopian calendar conversion | None |
| mailer | Email composition (user-initiated only) | None |

**None of these packages send data off your device.**

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

1. **View Data:** All your saved data is visible within the app
2. **Delete Individual Items:** Use delete buttons next to reminders and age records
3. **Clear All Data:** Use the "Clear All" option in the bookmarks section
4. **Uninstall:** Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

## Data Retention

- Data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
- Uninstalling the app deletes all associated data
- We have no ability to retain or recover your data
